# Neon Calendar

SwiftSlotz is a revolutionary Appointment Scheduling application aimed at providing the ultimate user-friendly experience. With a modern design, intuitive navigation, and a myriad of features, it redefines the way you organize and visualize your schedule.

## Features

1. **Custom Pie Chart**: Visualize your schedule in a fun, engaging way with our custom pie chart, which divides your day into easily distinguishable sectors.
2. **AM/PM Divider**: Schedules are divided into AM and PM sections for convenient and intuitive scheduling and visualization.
3. **Dynamic Color Coding**: Different sectors can be color-coded for easier identification and organization.
4. **Slot Creation**: Create slots in your schedule by simply touching the desired area on the pie chart.
5. **Drag and Drop Feature**: Adjust your schedules by dragging and dropping slots in the custom pie chart.

## Getting Started

To get a local copy up and running, follow these steps:

2. Install the required dependencies
3. Run the application
